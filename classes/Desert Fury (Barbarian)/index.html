    Desert Fury (Barbarian)

    The Sun-Scorched

    A desert nomad who enters a frenzy in battle, fueled by the scorching heat of Amun-Ra.

    Solar Fortitude

    At level 3, you gains resistance to fire damage and can add your Constitution modifier to your saving throws against fire-based effects.

    Pyre Strike

    At level 6, you gains the ability to imbue your attacks with flames. Whenever you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can choose to deal an additional 1d6 fire damage. This damage increases by 1d6 at 9th level and every three levels thereafter.

    Solar Flare

    At level 10, you gains the ability to unleash a burst of solar energy while in a rage. This burst deals 3d6 radiant damage to all creatures within a 10-foot radius, and also blinds them for one rounds. You can use this ability a number of times equal to your level divided by three rounding down, and it becomes available again after a long rest.

    Inferno Form

    At level 14, you gains the ability to transform into a being of pure flames while in a rage. While in this form your attacks deal an additional 3d4 fire damage, and you can use your reaction to make a burn weapon attack against that creature with 2d4 of damage.

    The Scrab

    A very tough desert nomad capable of ignoring its injuries and keep on fighting.

    Scrab Carapace

    At level 3, you gains a natural armor bonus of +6 to your AC, as your skin hardens and thickens like a scrab's exoskeleton. You also gain resistance to piercing damage.

    Scrab Defense

    At level 6, using your rage and determination to withstand the impact, you gains the ability to shrug off an attack. You can use this ability as a reaction when you are hit by a creature, and take no damage from it. This ability can be used once per long rest.

    Scrab Endurance

    At level 10, you gains the ability to tap into your inner reserves of strength and endurance, gaining 30 extra hit points for one encounter of your choice, or two at level 17. You can use your ability to withstand more damage, to push yourself beyond your normal limits, or to turn the tide of a battle in your favor. However, if you end the encounter with 30 or fewer hit points, you will fall into a coma and become unconscious. This ability can be used once per long rest.

    Scrab Invulnerability

    At level 14, you becomes nearly invulnerable to melee and ranged, non-magical attacks, gaining resistance to all forms of such damage. In addition, you gain the ability to shrug off critical hits, treating them as normal hits instead. However, you are still vulnerable to magical attacks and other forms of damage.
